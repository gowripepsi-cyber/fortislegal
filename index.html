Property Legal Due Diligence

Our premier property vetting process ensures absolute transaction safety by verifying ownership and structural compliance histories up to 30 years.

Scrutiny Checklist Includes:

  • Parent Document Review: We analyze every past registry transition deed to ensure a flawless unbroken ownership link.
  • Encumbrance Certificate Verification: Crosschecking with Sub-Registrar records to ensure zero mortgaged liabilities.
  • Layout & Approval Vetting: Vetting the property for statutory approvals (DTCP, CMDA, RERA) and checking agricultural zone classifications.
  • Revenue Records Trace: Matching descriptions with government Patta, Chitta, Adangal, and Field Measurement Sketches (FMB).
